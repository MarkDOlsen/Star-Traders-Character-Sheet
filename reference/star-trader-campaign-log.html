Season 1: The Quest for Janedhoe
01-01: The Smugglers of the Savage Void

The PCs are hired to transport a mysterious cargo across a rough frontier sector, but nothing goes as planned. Ambushed by pirates, they are forced to land on a savage, barely charted planet for repairs. There they encounter strange alien inhabitants who hold the key to getting their ship spaceworthy again - for a price. Once back in the void, the danger is not over: at their destination, a rival faction will stop at nothing to ensure the delivery never happens.

NOTE The PCs actually fought off the pirates pretty successfully, their ship better armed than the pirates were prepared for, and the crash landing aluded to above didn't happen in the actual play session.

01-02: The Apex Spire Heist

Looking for extra cash, the PCs are connected to Veln Corros, a buttoned-up corporate auditor who has discovered that his department's internal audits were covering up illegal research and smuggling operations involving the Obsidian Cartel and xeno-tech trafficking. He offers restricted information that could put the group onto the trail of the missing Jane Dhoe in exchange for help extracting a data vault from the Spire and faking his own death.

Following information Veln Corros pulled from the Spire's systems, the PCs travel to Xyros-6 to investigate the last known vector of The Transgressor, the ship they have learned the missing Jane Dhoe was traveling on. Locations

  • The Cove - Hidden in the Drosven System, a dark and gravitationally warped region near the edge of the Fringe. The system is threaded with unstable wormholes - both natural and artificial - held in place by alien latticework and pirate-installed control nodes. Dark, cold, and lit by gravitic lensing and blue ion trails, it serves as the hub of the Crimson Pirates' ghost-lane network. A wrong calculation can mean lost ships, torn hulls, or vanished crews.
    1. Dock Pit 6 - A rusted pressure lock hangar surrounded by stacked cargo crates, broken drones, and airlock consoles overseen by Dockmaster Kreel Zann. The atmosphere is dominated by oil-slicked floor plating and faint music from a distant bar. Gun-toting crew watch all newcomers.
    2. The Chainbone - A half-sunken chamber in a zero-G area of the station. Pirates gather here to drink, duel, and gamble.
    3. The Slagforge - Ramshackle tech market inside an old refinery chamber. Full of black market modders, alien tech dealers, and broken AIs offering services.
    4. The Cathedral - The throne-room-like command center is where the three Captains meet. Built into a hollowed Sladrallan structure, reconfigured with pirate war tech. Guards in repurposed combat suits keep outsiders out unless cleared. The hall is dim, lit by glowing Sladrallan glyphs and old signal fire braziers. A triangular dais rises, where each of the Captains sit on a throne made from scavenged ship hulls and relics. Armed retainers and whispering acolytes line the chamber edges.

NPCs

  • Redmother Vahira - One of the three leaders of the Crimson Pirates, a Sladrallan mystic and keeper of the pirates' wormhole lore.
  • High Captain Luro Thresk - The pirates' military leader, a brutal and disciplined Hacragorkan loyal only to pirate survival.
  • Sol Vex - A Ken Reeg negotiator, trader, and handler for the pirates. Silver-tongued, sleazy, and too ambitious for his own good.
  • Threx Vorga - A noble hunter and self-exile from Augustus' homeworld who now works as an enforcer and bounty hunter for the pirates. She seems to have a garbled understanding of why Augustus is so far from home and may know something about his missing brother.
