Delvers

Massacre monsters, take their stuff!

Delvers is a high-octane, episodic hack-and-slash fantasy campaign that strips away complex politics and deep lore to focus on action, danger, and loot. Players are professional adventurers who descend into sprawling, monster-infested megadungeons to defeat deadly foes and haul out as much treasure as possible.

Expect brutal combat, dangerous traps, careful inventory management, and the thrill of uncovering wildly overpowered magical gear as you carve a path through one lethal challenge after another in pursuit of fame and fortune.

The Doors to Infinity

Open the Door. Explore Infinity.

Doors to Infinity is a Fate hack inspired by Lords of Gossamer and Shadow, set across an endless multiverse of parallel realities known as the Gossamer worlds.

These worlds are separated by the Shadow, an infinite, howling void of nonexistence, and connected by the Grand Stair—a vast labyrinth of stairways, corridors, and mystical Doors that lead from one universe to another.

Players take the roles of Wardens of the Grand Stair, rare individuals gifted with the power to perceive and open these Doors. Where others see only an ordinary doorway, a Warden sees a path to wonder, danger, intrigue, and worlds beyond imagination.

Earthdawn, Fate of Barsaive

They survived the darkness. Now they must reclaim the world.

A Fate hack of Earthdawn, this is a high-fantasy, post-apocalyptic adventure set in the world of Barsaive, centuries after the magical cataclysm known as The Scourge forced civilization underground into protective citadels called Kaers.

Now the great races—Humans, Trolls, Dwarfs, T’skrang, and others—emerge as Adepts, heroes who channel raw magic into every Discipline, from swordplay to sorcery. Players will brave lost Kaers, recover thread-linked magical artifacts and forgotten lore, and fight for the future of Barsaive against the iron grip of the Theran Empire.

Fate of Swanharbor

Classic D&D Fantasy in a Small Coastal Town.

Fate of Swanharbor is a grounded, classic fantasy setting rooted in the small coastal town of Swanharbor, the first line of defense against the dangers of the vast, abandoned interior.

The campaign centers on everyday survival in a world of fragile civilization, local intrigue, ancient secrets left behind by the fallen Empire of Nimduin, and the constant need to protect the community from magical threats, wild spirits, and wilderness terrors lurking just beyond the town’s borders.

Heirs of Olympus

Godhood is a mess. Welcome to the family.

Heirs of Olympus is a modern urban fantasy divine telenovela where players become newly awakened deities, chosen by the returning Divine Spark of the ancient gods, while still trying to survive ordinary life in modern Chicago.

This campaign thrives on high-stakes melodrama: rivalries, betrayals, love triangles, family complications, and mythic power struggles—as characters learn to control their reality-bending divine gifts while balancing godhood with rent, work, and the need to hide their true nature from the mortal world.

The Infinite Sea

Gold, glory, and piracy on the high seas.

The Infinite Sea is a mythic pirate adventure set on a flat world of eternal water, blending the romantic grit of the Age of Sail with active mythology and magic. Players are part of a ship’s crew, chasing treasure, raiding rivals, and navigating the perilous waters between the Known Seas and the mysterious Outer Currents.

Expect swashbuckling action, strange god-touched shores, dangerous voyages, and encounters with minor deities and colossal god-beasts that make every journey feel like a brush with legend.

Star Traders

Keep your blasters ready and the engine running.

Star Traders is a classic science fantasy space opera set in The Fringe, the lawless outer frontier of the galaxy, where players are smugglers, freebooters, and daring adventurers piloting a beloved but barely functional starship.

The campaign focuses on pulp-style action, moral gray areas, high-stakes smuggling and trade runs, exotic alien species, espionage, and double-crosses as the crew tries to pay their debts and strike it rich before bad luck or major factions finally catch up to them.
